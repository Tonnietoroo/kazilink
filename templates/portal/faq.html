Frequently Asked Questions

Kazilink is a neighborhood services directory that connects local service providers (such as electricians, tutors, carpenters, and plumbers) with customers in their community. Users can browse service providers, read reviews, and even book services through the platform.

To become a service provider on Kazilink, simply create a job profile by providing details about the services you offer, your location, and any relevant certifications or qualifications. Once your profile is created, you can start posting jobs and connecting with potential clients.

To book a service provider, browse the directory, view the service provider's profile, and select a service you want to book. You can then contact them through the platform to finalize the details, such as scheduling and pricing.

Yes! After using a service, you can leave a review based on your experience. This helps others in your community make informed decisions when selecting a service provider.

Kazilink is expanding and aims to serve multiple regions. You can check the available service providers in your area by browsing the directory. If you are a service provider, you can sign up regardless of your location.

You can contact Kazilink support by visiting the Contact page or by sending an email to support@kazilink.com. Our team will respond to your inquiries promptly.
